Workshop Day: September 26th, 2025 - Room D4 (O2 universum)

Program

    13:30 – 13:45

    Opening and General Setting (Organizers)

    13:45 – 15:00

    Session 1

    • "A Holistic View of Sustainability in Multimodal Recommender Systems" – Angelo Geninatti Cossatin, Liliana Ardissono and Noemi Mauro (12 min + 5 Q&A)
    • "Sustainability Evaluation Metrics for Recommender Systems" – Alexander Felfernig, Damian Garber, Viet-Man Le, Sebastian Lubos and Thi Ngoc Trang Tran (12 min + 5 Q&A)
    • "Towards Greener Choices: Decision Information Nudging for Sustainability-Aware Recommender Explanations" – Haya Halimeh and Oliver Müller (12 min + 5 Q&A)
    • "Estimating Product Carbon Footprint via Large Language Models for Sustainable Recommender Systems" – Alessandro Vicenti, Cataldo Musto, Giuseppe Spillo, Allegra De Filippo, Michela Milano and Giovanni Semeraro (12 min + 5 Q&A)

    15:00 – 15:30

    Spotlight Presentations

    • "TRUST-MH: Transparent and Responsible User-level Semantic Tagging for Mental Health Assessment" – Sumit Dalal and Sarika Jain (4 min)
    • "SERMuse: Speech Emotion Recognition based Music Recommendation" – Bhawna Singh and Niyati Baliyan (4 min)
    • "LLM-based Healthiness? Analyzing the Nutritional Quality of an AI-Generated Recipe Dataset" – Ayoub El Majjodi, Jonas Bech Holtan, Alain D. Starke and Christoph Trattner (4 min)
    • "Diversified Recommendations of Cultural Activities with Personalized Determinantal Point Processes" – Carole Ibrahim, Hiba Bederina, Daniel Cuesta, Laurent Montier, Cyrille Delabre and Jill-Jênn Vie (4 min)
    • "LLM-Powered Recommendations: a Case Study in Renewable Energy Communities" – Bianca Maria Deconcini, Giulia Coucourde, Luca Console, Michele Visciola, Malek Anouti and Giorgio Gaudio (4 min)
    • "Mind the Gap: Urban-Rural Disparities in Wheelchair Accessibility for POI Recommendations" – Gregor Mayr, Markus Reiter-Haas and Elisabeth Lex (4 min)

    15:30 – 16:00

    Coffee break & Poster session

    16:00 – 17:15

    Session 2

    • "Early Explorations of Recommender Systems for Physical Activity and Wellbeing" – Alan Said (8 min + 5 Q&A)
    • "E-Mealio: An LLM-Powered Conversational Agent for Sustainable and Healthy Food Recommendation" – Antonio Raffaele Iacovazzi, Lorenzo Blanco, Giuseppe Spillo and Cataldo Musto (12 min + 5 Q&A)
    • "Biases of Algorithmic and Editorial Recommendations in Public Service Media" – Marcel Hauck and Franz Rothlauf (12 min + 5 Q&A)
    • "PureBiasoMeter: Decoupling Popularity Bias from User Fairness in LLM-Based Recommender Systems" – Shirin Tahmasebinotarki, Muhammad Hamad, Amir H. Payberah and Mihhail Matskin (12 min + 5 Q&A)

    17:15 – 17:30

    Closing & Final Remarks (Organizers)
